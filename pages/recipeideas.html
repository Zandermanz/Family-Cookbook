  • Baked Chicken
    • Whole chicken
    • Kosher salt- ¼ cup
    • Pepper
    • Lemons- 2
    • Garlic- 6-8 cloves peeled
    • Rosemary- several sprigs or Thyme- one sprig.
    • Butter- 6 Tbsp.
    1. 8 to 24 hours before baking dry brine by rubbing chicken with salt and pepper under the skin of the breast and let sit covered in the fridge.
    2. Quarter lemons, peel garlic, and stuff in cavity of chicken along with the herb you are using.
    3. Add seasoning under skin: Cut butter into cubes, smear some of the butter under the skin along with reserved herbs and slivered garlic cloves
    4. Rub outside of the chicken with remaining butter and herbs.
    5. Roast at 350 with aluminum foil tent for the first 30 minutes. Remove tent and cook for another 30-60 minutes, checking temperature with a meat thermometer.
  • Creamed Mushrooms
    • Minced Onion- 2 Tbsp.
    • Butter- 2 Tbsp.
    • Brandy- 1 Tbsp.
    • Sherry- 1 Tbsp.
    • Mushrooms- 1 to 2 pounds any fresh mushroom
    • Cream- 1
    • Flour- 1 Tbsp.
    1. Melt butter in skillet, add onion, saute until soft
    2. Add quartered mushrooms to the pan and saute over high heat until moisture evaporates
    3. Sprinkle top with 1 Tbsp. flour
    4. Add salt, pepper, and cream
    5. Simmer briefly and then add brandy and sherry
    6. Correct seasoning as needed and serve
  • Pork Chops, Apples, & Onions
    • Pork chops- 2
    • Apples cut into wedges- 2
    • Onions- 1
    • Apple Cider- ½ -1 Cup
    • Salt and Pepper to taste
    • Raisins (optional)
    1. Season pork chops with salt and pepper.
    2. Warm olive oil pan.
    3. Brown pork chops on each side.
    4. Add apples and onions, and raisins if using.
    5. Add apple cider and cover pan.
    6. Cook until pork chops are to temp and apples are tender
