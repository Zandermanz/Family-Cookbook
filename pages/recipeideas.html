  • Creamed Mushrooms
    • Minced Onion- 2 Tbsp.
    • Butter- 2 Tbsp.
    • Brandy- 1 Tbsp.
    • Sherry- 1 Tbsp.
    • Mushrooms- 1 to 2 pounds any fresh mushroom
    • Cream- 1
    • Flour- 1 Tbsp.
    1. Melt butter in skillet, add onion, saute until soft
    2. Add quartered mushrooms to the pan and saute over high heat until moisture evaporates
    3. Sprinkle top with 1 Tbsp. flour
    4. Add salt, pepper, and cream
    5. Simmer briefly and then add brandy and sherry
    6. Correct seasoning as needed and serve
  • Pork Chops, Apples, & Onions
