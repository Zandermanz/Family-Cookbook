C'est Colin's Puttanesca

Ingredients

  • Pasta- Linguine or Buccatini- 1 Pound
  • Anchovies- 6 Fillets
  • Garlic- 4 Cloves- Minced
  • Tomatoes- San Marazono -Large 28 oz can
  • Tomato Paste- 1 tspn
  • Wine- 1 Tbsp
  • Olives- 1 Cup - quartered, half green and half morrocan or other oil cured olive
  • Capers- 3 Tbsp
  • Olive Oil
  • Panko Bread Crumbs- 1/2 Cup
  • Parsley
  • Pepper and Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Dice Anchovies-
  2. Add olive oil to hot pan
  3. Add anchovies and garlic, sautee until fragrant
  4. Add Tomato paste and wine and simmer for a few minutes
  5. Add Tomatoes, simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Add quartered olives and capers, including a 1/2 teaspoon of caper water and cook for another 10 minutes
  7. Check for salt
  8. Cook Pasta
  9. Toss pasta with sauce and add a glug of olive oil, pepper, and a half a cup of Panko bread crumbs
  10. Shower with parmesean cheese, dizzle with olive oil, add a sprinkel of parsley, and serve
