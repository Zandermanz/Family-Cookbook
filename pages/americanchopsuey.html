American Chop Suey

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of pasta.
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 2 cans of condensed tomato soup
  • 2 cans of stewed tomatoes
  • Salt to taste.

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in boiling salted water according to package directions or until tender.
  2. Brown ground beef.
  3. Add soup and tomatoes to beef and simmer. If you do not have condensed tomato soup, add regular tomato soup with added sugar
  4. Add cooked pasta and stir
